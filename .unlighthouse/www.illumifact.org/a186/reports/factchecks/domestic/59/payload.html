Rwanda, a deterrent policy or a political ploy?

Published at 30 April 2024

What was claimed

The government's Rwanda scheme will reduce net migration levels and deter migrants from trying to enter the UK.

Summary of our analysis

The scheme is more of a political ploy than a real scheme. The government is not addressing the root of the problem, which appears to be a poor policy for asylum seekers already in place that compels desperate asylum seekers to use what the government believes are illegal means.
